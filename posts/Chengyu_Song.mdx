Dr. Song earned his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Georgia Tech, supervised by professor Wenke Lee and Taesoo Kim.

His research interests include system security, program analysis and verification, and operating systems including

- Advancing techniques for finding vulnerabilities in binaries, OS kernels, machine learning applications, and smart grid.
- Eliminating vulnerabilities through automatic patch generation and verification.
- New exploit techniques and automated exploit generation.
- Runtime exploit prevention with software hardware co-design.
