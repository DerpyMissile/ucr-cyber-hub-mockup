A Professor at University of California, Riverside affiliated with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering and the RAISE Institute. 
From 2013 to 2023, served as Director of the Academic Center of Excellence in Cyber Security Research (ACE-CSR) and Head of the Information Security Research Group (ISRG) in the University College London (UCL).

In September 2023, he started the Security and Privacy Advanced research Lab (SPALab). 
He got my PhD from UC Irvine, advised by Gene Tsudik. 
While in grad school, he did internships at NEC in Heidelberg (2008), INRIA Rhone-Alpes (2009), and Nokia in Lausanne (2010). 
After graduating, he worked as a Research Scientist at Xerox PARC from 2011 to 2013.
