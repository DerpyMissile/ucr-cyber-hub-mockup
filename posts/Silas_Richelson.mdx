Professor Richelson is an Assistant Professor in the CSE Department at UC Riverside. 
His research interests are in Cryptography, Computer Security and Complexity Theory. 
He got my Ph. D. in 2014 from UCLA under the supervision of Rafail Ostrovsky. 
From 2015 to 2017 he was a postdoctoral researcher with a joint appointment at the MIT CSAIL and BU Computer Science departments, working with Vinod Vaikuntanathan and Ran Canetti.
